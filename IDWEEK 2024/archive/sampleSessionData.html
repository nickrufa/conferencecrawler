Learning Objectives:

At the conclusion of this session, participants will be able to:
  • describe changes in gut microbiota in cancer patients;
  • recognize the role of various drugs and antibiotics on changes of gut microbiota;
  • know the relationship between mucin-degrading bacteria and fever in severely neutropenic patients;
  • explain the impact of gut microbiota on complications and outcomes in cancer patients.
