Learning Objectives:

At the conclusion of this session, participants will be able to:
  • describe the current landscape of emerging infectious diseases in sub-saharan africa and the americas;
  • discuss strategies for anticipating and mitigating zoonotic spillover events;
  • evaluate the barriers and opportunities in developing effective diagnostics for emerging infections.
